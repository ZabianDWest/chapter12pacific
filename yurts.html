Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort

What is a yurt?

A yurt is a circular tent traditionally used by nomads in Central Asia. Our yurts are modern and luxurious, with comfortable furnishings and stunning views of the surrounding nature.

How are the yurts finished?

Our yurts are equipped with comfortable beds, heating, and electricity. Each yurt also has a private deck with seating, perfect for enjoying the beautiful surroundings.

What should I bring?

Bring appropriate clothing for the weather and any personal items you might need during your stay.
